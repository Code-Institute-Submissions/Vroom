About

Our mission

We want to help people to have safe access to the experience of driving high end cars and to offer high spec driving solutions.

Origins

Vroom came from the observed need of high end car rental.

We started with a couple of classic cars being rented for weddings and other special occasions.

After a couple of months, it became common to have users looking for other cars that we didn't own.

So we decided to open up the car supply to other users and got to the model we have today.

Founder

Carson McCarr is the proud owner of the two cars that started it all.

He grew up fixing farm equipment with his father and building his own machines on his spare time.

He bought the two cars as scrap metal and took the commitment to bring them back to life.

McCarr is 96 years old and still goes every day to his garage to work on his restoration projects.

The Convertible Roadster

Fist car ever bought by McCarr. Found in a scrapyard rotting away.

This car was swapped for an old shovel with a neighbour farmer.

Not many believed this car would ever be back on the road but not McCarr.

After years of work and gathering parts, this piece of history is back.

The Wedding Pearl

McCarr bought this car in 1972 as a project to work on.

Nicknamed Wedding Pearl by one of the happy customers, the name resounded with the character of the car and seems to have stuck to it.

This car cost him a dozen free range eggs. It had no wheels, no doors, no engine. Most parts were missing.

Most people would regret giving away that dozen free range eggs but not McCarr.

Took him 5 years to find all parts for the car and now it is worth a bit more than that dozen eggs.
